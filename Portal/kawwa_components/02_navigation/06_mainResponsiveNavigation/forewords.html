Use this component for your main site navigation.

This component can have two levels of navigation (via a dropdown menu) and it is fully responsive.

Notice that, on the demo here below, you will see how the menu will look like depending on the size of your browser's window or device.

Don't forget to check and adjust, if necessary, the level of the header (h tag). You may use The HTML5 Outliner to check and finetune your document outline.
